About Us

Titan Community Bank was founded in 1975 by a group of local business owners who were frustrated with the lack of personal attention and support they were receiving from their big-name banks. They felt that the banks were more interested in profits than in helping their customers succeed, and they knew that they needed a better banking partner if they were going to thrive.

The group came together to form a new kind of bank – one that would be owned and operated by members of the community, and that would prioritize personalized service and support for local businesses and individuals. They pooled their resources and expertise to create Titan Community Bank, which quickly became known as the go-to bank for anyone looking for a personal touch and a commitment to their success.

In the early years, Titan Community Bank operated out of a small storefront on Main Street, where the founders themselves would greet customers and handle transactions. As the bank grew, it expanded its offerings and opened additional branches throughout the community, but it never lost sight of its mission to serve the people and businesses of the community.

Today, Titan Community Bank is a thriving institution with a strong reputation for excellence in banking and a deep commitment to its customers and the community. It continues to be locally owned and operated, and its leadership remains dedicated to the principles that guided its founding – personalized service, community involvement, and a focus on the success of its customers.
